1. Introduction

Oceanus Tech ("we", "us", or "our") built TrackMySpend as a freemium mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By creating an account or using TrackMySpend, you explicitly acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy, including the transfer of your data to servers located outside your country of residence. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information — your name, email address, and profile photo (optional) when you register or sign in via Google
  • Expense data — expense titles, amounts, categories, dates, notes, and payment methods you enter into the app
  • Workspace names — labels you create to organise your expenses
  • Budget settings — monthly budget limits and category budgets you configure

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information — device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage data — app features used, screens visited, and session duration, collected via Firebase Analytics
  • Crash reports — error logs and stack traces collected via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs
  • Advertising ID — a resettable device identifier used by Google AdMob to show personalised ads to free users only

2.3 Biometric Data

TrackMySpend offers optional biometric login (fingerprint or Face ID). We want to be clear: Oceanus Tech does not collect, receive, transmit, or store any biometric data whatsoever. Biometric authentication is handled entirely and exclusively by your device's operating system (Android BiometricManager API). Your fingerprint or facial data never leaves your device and never reaches our servers.

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect bank account numbers, credit card numbers, or any financial credentials
  • We do not access your contacts, SMS messages, or call logs
  • We do not collect your precise or approximate location
  • We do not collect biometric data (see Section 2.3 above)

4. Cross-Border Data Transfer and Storage

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore and related services), with data centres located primarily in the United States. By creating an account and using TrackMySpend, you explicitly and actively consent to your personal data being transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States and other countries where Google operates infrastructure.

These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country of residence. Google Firebase maintains appropriate safeguards for such transfers including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.

Firebase provides industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256).

5. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • Authentication is required to access your account
  • Firestore security rules ensure each user can only access their own data
  • Release builds are minified and obfuscated to deter reverse engineering
  • We use Firebase App Check to prevent unauthorised access to our backend

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by applicable law — within 72 hours for GDPR-regulated users and within the timeframes required by the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA) for Indian users.

7. Advertising

Free users of TrackMySpend may see banner and interstitial advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your Advertising ID to show personalised ads based on your interests.

Premium subscribers do not see any advertisements.

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time:

  • Android 12 and above: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID
  • Android 11 and below: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation

8. Data Retention

  • Your account and expense data is retained as long as your account is active
  • If you delete your account through the app (Settings → Delete Account), all your personal data is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our Firebase database within 30 days
  • Anonymised, aggregated analytics data (which cannot be linked back to you) may be retained longer for product improvement purposes
  • Crash logs are automatically purged by Firebase after 90 days

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

All users:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated personal data
  • Portability — export your expense data directly in CSV or Excel format from the Reports screen in the app at any time

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom:

You have additional rights under the GDPR and UK GDPR, including the right to restrict processing, object to processing, and lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

Users in India:

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA), you have the right to access, correct, and erase your personal data, and to grievance redressal. Contact us at support@oceanustech.in for any grievances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@oceanustech.in. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at support@oceanustech.in and we will delete that information promptly.
